Bio

I am currently a fifth-year doctoral student in computer science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University（上海交通大学）, supervised by Prof. Liqing Zhang（张丽清教授）and Prof. Li Niu（牛力教授）. My research interests lie in generative AI and computer vision, especially in image/video generation and editing.

Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree in computer science of Zhiyuan Honors Program (also known as ACM Class), Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Yong Yu（俞勇教授）and Prof. Junchi Yan（严骏驰教授）.
